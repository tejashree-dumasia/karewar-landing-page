ABOUT US

The Construction Expert

Karewar Infracon has been providing construction services since 2011. We've completed a variety of successful Residential, Commercial and Government projects in Pune & Parbhani with 100% client satisfaction.

WE are ISO 9001:2015 certified company, Our highly trained and experienced team is committed to completing your project with expertise and precision.

At KAREWAR Infracon, we understand that building a home or business space is more than just bricks and wood. It's about creating a place for you to call your own for years to come. We value your trust and always strive for excellence in our work.
